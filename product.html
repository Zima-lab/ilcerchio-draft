EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL

VYNEGAR

Our Vinegar is produced using traditional static fermentation in open barriques and left to rest for months at 29°C. With a bold character and intense aromas, it's perfect for artichoke salads, braised red meats, game, and chocolate-based desserts.
